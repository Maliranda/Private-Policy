Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

Introduction

AI Product Review Analyzer ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Chrome browser extension.

Information We Collect

1. Website Content

When you use the Extension to analyze product reviews, we access and process:

  • Product Reviews: Text content from customer reviews on product pages you visit
  • Product Information: Product names, prices, and ratings from pages you actively analyze

This information is only accessed when you explicitly click the "Analyze Reviews" button on a product page. We do not automatically collect content from pages you visit.

2. User Actions

We collect anonymous usage analytics to provide you with a personal analytics dashboard:

  • Number of analyses performed
  • Dates and times of analyses
  • Button clicks and extension interactions

This data is stored locally in your browser and anonymously in our analytics database. No personal identification information is included.

3. Price History Data

If you use the price tracking feature, we store product price information locally in your browser:

  • Product prices over time
  • Product URLs you've analyzed
  • Currency information

This data is stored only on your device using Chrome's local storage and is never transmitted to our servers.

What We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Authentication credentials or passwords
  • Financial or payment information
  • Medical information
  • Your browsing history (except for product pages you explicitly request analysis for)
  • Personal communications

How We Use Your Information

Core Functionality

  • AI Analysis: Product reviews you request analysis for are sent to OpenAI's API to generate pros/cons summaries. This is essential for the Extension's core functionality.
  • Analytics Dashboard: Usage statistics are used to provide you with a personal analytics dashboard showing your analysis history.
  • Price Tracking: Product price data is stored locally to show price history trends.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: Processes review text to generate AI-powered analysis. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data sent to their service.
  • Supabase: Stores anonymous usage analytics. Data is anonymized and contains no personal information.
  • Admitad & AliExpress Affiliate Programs: When you click product links on supported shopping sites, affiliate parameters may be added to URLs. These services handle their own data collection according to their privacy policies.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Price history and some analytics are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage APIs. This data remains on your device.
  • Cloud Storage: Anonymous usage analytics are stored in Supabase. All data is anonymized and contains no personal identifiers.
  • Data Retention: Analytics data is retained for up to 1 year. You can clear local data at any time through Chrome's extension settings.
  • Security: We use industry-standard security measures to protect data in transit and at rest.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your data. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With OpenAI (for AI analysis) and Supabase (for analytics storage) as necessary for the Extension to function.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Control Data Collection: You can stop using the Extension at any time. No data collection occurs unless you actively use the Extension.
  • Clear Local Data: You can clear extension data through Chrome's settings: chrome://extensions → AI Product Review Analyzer → Details → Remove extension data.
  • Opt-Out: You can disable the Extension at any time to stop all data collection.

Children's Privacy

Our Extension is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Extension after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

This Extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements
